There are four major driving forces behind evolution.

  • Genetic drift
  • Mutation
  • Natural selection
  • Migration
Two more forces, namely culture and population mating structure, are new in the sense that they are not explicated in traditional texts. For more details on the four forces, check this out.

In the following pages, we shall explore the effects of genetic drift and mutation. In nature, all these forces donot operate in individual vacuums. Instead, they have dynamic interactions.
